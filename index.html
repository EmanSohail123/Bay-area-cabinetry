HDI Premium Collection

Our flagship cabinet line represents the pinnacle of craftsmanship, featuring solid wood construction with hand-cut dovetail drawer boxes and premium, low-VOC finishes. Each piece in the HDI collection is meticulously handcrafted in our Iowa workshop using sustainably sourced North American hardwoods.

Urban Modern Series

The Urban Modern Series redefines contemporary kitchen design with its high-gloss lacquer finishes, seamless handle-less cabinetry, and innovative storage solutions. Featuring German-engineered hinges with 175° opening angles and integrated LED lighting options.

J&K Handcrafted

Our J&K Handcrafted collection brings traditional woodworking techniques into the modern home, with each cabinet door and drawer front individually hand-planed, sanded, and finished by our master cabinetmakers. Featuring mortise-and-tenon joinery, hand-applied milk paint or stain finishes, and custom distressing options, these pieces develop a beautiful patina over time.

About Bay Area Cabinetry

Founded in 2018 by a proud St Petersburg native, Bay Area Cabinetry was built on a passion for exceptional craftsmanship and a dedication to bringing every client’s vision to life.

From the first consultation to the final installation, we take the time to understand your needs and bring functionality to your space that blends beauty and lasting quality. Every project is handled with the precision and care it deserves.

Whether you’re updating a bathroom or creating your dream kitchen, we’re here to deliver cabinetry you’ll love for years to come.
